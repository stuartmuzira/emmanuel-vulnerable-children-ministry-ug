About Us

Empowering Lives, Restoring Hope in Uganda.

Emmanuel Vulnerable Children Ministry Uganda (EVCM) is a faith-based non-profit organization dedicated to supporting vulnerable children, widows, and disadvantaged families across Uganda. We provide access to education, shelter, healthcare, and spiritual guidance to transform lives and build sustainable futures.
