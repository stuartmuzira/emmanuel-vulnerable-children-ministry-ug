Children Are Our Future

Mama Nakato

Single Mother of 4

"My children used to go to bed hungry every night. Now they eat posho, beans, and greens daily at the feeding center. My eldest just got school fees paid and is top of his class!"

Papa Musoke

Guardian of 3 Orphans

"I lost my sister and took her 3 children. EVCM pays their school fees, feeds them, and teaches them tailoring. They call me their hero, but EVCM are the real heroes!"

Grandmother of 5 Grandchildren

"At 68 years old, I raise 5 grandchildren alone. EVCM gives them food, education, and church. I cry tears of joy seeing them healthy and learning God's word."

Sarah K.
