Our Mission

Empowering Lives, Restoring Hope in Uganda

Emmanuel Vulnerable Children Ministry Uganda (EVCM) is a registered faith-based non-profit organization founded in 2015, dedicated to transforming the lives of vulnerable children, widows, elderly, and disadvantaged families across Uganda. Operating from our headquarters in Kavule Village, Kamuli District, we provide holistic support through education sponsorships, nutrition programs, skills development training, healthcare access, and spiritual guidance.

Our vision is to create sustainable pathways out of poverty by equipping individuals with knowledge, skills, and hope. With a team of 25 dedicated staff and over 100 volunteers, EVCM serves communities in Kamuli, Jinja, and Kampala districts, focusing on orphan care, single parent support, and youth empowerment initiatives.
