Daily Feeding Program

Nourishing 1,200 Vulnerable Children Daily

Through 5 community feeding centers and home delivery, we provide balanced daily meals to orphans, vulnerable children, and widows. Our sustainable farming ensures fresh, nutritious food year-round.

Every meal contains proteins, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals essential for growth, learning, and health. Zero malnutrition cases among our beneficiaries.
