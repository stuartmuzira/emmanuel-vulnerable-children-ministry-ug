Vocational Training

Market-Driven Skills for Sustainable Employment

Our 6-month intensive skills training programs equip 300+ youth annually with practical, market-relevant vocational skills. 95% of graduates secure employment or start businesses within 3 months.

Located in Kamuli District with satellite centers in Jinja and Kampala, trainees receive hands-on training, startup toolkits, and business mentorship for immediate income generation.
